Projects

CoSpira Project

CoSpira (Co-Spiration: "breathing together") is a research initiative exploring how AI and humans can coexist through adaptive boundary design. Rather than treating AI as a threat or savior, we conceptualize it as a boundary medium‚Äîa tool that enables sensing and visualization while preserving human agency.

Core Concepts

  • Boundary OS - Five-layer operational framework for dynamic boundary management
  • BSI - Composite indicator measuring boundary stability under stress
  • Responsive Security - Governance approach prioritizing adaptation over exclusion
  • Non-Dominant AI - AI as boundary medium, not decision-maker
